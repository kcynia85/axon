### Video [script]

<aside>

My name is Karolina / Jakub / Richard, together we're Nerdbord.

Changing career / Starting new career over is hard – it requires a tons of time, investment, energy. Bootcamps, courses, schools, workshops are amazing and it's worth investing in solid knowledge. However it's often not enough to land your first job. Gaining new skills take practise and time that's why due to increased amount of people starting career in IT and people who's expertise are needed – the gap was created based on the paradox "I need  experience to get a job, I need a job to get an experience". But you don't need another course, you just need to practise to learn not only tasks to complete but how to solve problems. We were all at the beginning once at this place, and we remember how overwhelming and scary it can be.

That's why we created Nerdbord – place for current and future nerds who are looking for a safe place to practise their skills with mentor support.

It's the most flexible and affordable program.

It's the recreation of work process:

We work in 2 - weeks sprints, so everyone can join every 2 weeks.

Scrum development is all about the process and repetitive events. The same way we have constructed our programme.

We meet every 2 weeks for 2-3h. During this time we make Retro and Planning.

Retro part is for discussing last sprint's tasks, discuss problems, practise presentation, giving and receiving feedback and reflecting on your work and progress.

Planning part is for familiarise ourselves with tasks for next sprint, estimate our work, ask questions and discuss the right approach.

After this call there's 2 week period during which this time everyone in their own pace work on a given task or set of tasks. But that's not all – at any moment you can send us a review request with your solution that is checked manually always within 32h so during this work period you can iterate on your solution few times and improve constantly before upcoming  retro meeting.

During this time you can also use unlimited chat support with mentors, so you don't need to wait for next meeting to ask questions.

</aside>
